About Me

My name is David Semedo, and I'm a Ph.D. Student at FCT-NOVA, from the NOVA University of Lisbon. I am a researcher of the Web and Media Search group (NOVA Search), at the NOVA Laboratory for Computer Science and Informatics (NOVA LINCS). My advisor is Professor João Magalhães.

My current work focuses on Temporal Cross-modal Retrieval and Neural-based Representation Learning.

Research interests: Cross-modal retrieval, Representation Learning, Deep Learning, Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence.

Academic degrees:

2015 M.Sc. in Computer Science by NOVA University of Lisbon [Thesis] [Bib] under the supervision of Prof. Pedro Barahona and Prof. Pedro Medeiros.

2013 B.Sc. in Computer Science by NOVA University of Lisbon.
